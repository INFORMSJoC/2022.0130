This fold inclues four files, realworld, synthetic, weighted_realworld and weighted_synthetic.
(1) "realworld" fold consists of 26 networks from practical applications
(2) "synthetic" fold consists of 16 instances generated based on four famous complex network models
(3) "weighted_realworld" fold consists of 26 weighted instances, which are generated based on realworld CNP benchmark
(4) "weighted_synthetic" fold consists of 16 weighted instances, which are genrated based on the synthetic CNP benchmark
